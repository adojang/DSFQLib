DSFQ OR Cell with Rylov's DSFQ Loop with Dual Time Constants Characterisation Information


/* Characteristics */

Designed Skew Tolerance: 15ps
True Skew Tolerance: 7ps
Cell Propagation Time - The time bewteen which the inputs are 4.44 RAD (0.707 * 2pi) and the output is 4.44 RAD
Cell Propagation Time for Single Input: ~3.85ps 
Cell Propagation Time for Double Input no Skew: ~3.3ps
Cell Propagation Time for two stacked OR gates with single input: ~9 ps

Average Propagation Time: ~3.57ps
Mininum Time between sucessive 0 skew inputs: 9ps

Reccomended Time Between Inputs: 15ps - this is what the gate was optimized for.

/* Margins */

Critical Margin: 25.8% for 15ps skew
Critical  Component   (I1 -)
Margin of 20ps: 23.3% (I1 +)
Margin of 15ps: 25.8% (I1 -)
Margin of 10ps: 22.9% (I1 +)

!WARNING!
The cell may not operate reliably at at a skew tolerance of 9ps or less.
